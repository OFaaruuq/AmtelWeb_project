About Us

Helping You Achieve Seamless Communication

At AMTEL Ltd, we are dedicated to providing top-quality telecommunications services to individuals, businesses, and government entities across Somalia. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we deliver reliable solutions that empower communities and connect the nation.

Our services include mobile voice and data, fixed voice and data, messaging, mobile money services (MyCash), cloud solutions, and broadband internet. We are committed to enhancing connectivity, enabling progress, and making communication seamless and accessible.
